Beetlejuice-Inspired Shrimp Cocktail

This Beetlejuice-inspired shrimp cocktail is a perfect appetizer for any occasion.

The spicy homemade aioli is what sets this shrimp cocktail apart from all the rest.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shrimp, deveined, shell on
  • 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 poblano pepper, seeded
  • 1/2 cup grapeseed or canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 6 to 8 fresh basil leaves
